Meet Rob

Rob Tompkins is a software engineer, open‑source leader at the Apache Software Foundation, and a lifelong Virginian. He’s also a caregiver who navigated our health system firsthand after a family accident. Rob believes government should be an engine for opportunity — practical, transparent, and on your side.

  • • Builder: Led cloud & security projects used by millions.
  • • Caregiver: 15+ years advocating for affordable, quality home care.
  • • Community: From STEM mentorship to veterans’ support.

Family & Roots

Rob is a lifelong Virginian raising his family here in the Richmond area. From weekends on the James to weeknights helping with homework, the Tompkins family keeps Rob grounded in what matters: safe neighborhoods, great schools, and an economy that works for everyone.
