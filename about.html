About Me

I'm Christopher Robinson Tompkins III (now that's a mouthful, so I go by Rob). My family helped bring you the United States. That's right folks the Tompkins were there in 1776. Now, over the years we made mistakes along the way, did both bad and good things. We were on the wrong side of Civil War, but then turned around and helped make VCU medical center (MCV at the time) a national name in medicine. We helped win World War II, helped in the Eisenhower administration, and helped keep our environment cleaner (my father was an Environmental Engineer, helping to protect our environment).

Over my years I've been on the US Canoe and Kayak Team, done ground-breaking research in Mathematics, been a caretaker for the disabled, written music, and built and maintained software that runs in millions of applications around the world. I've even helped NASA with their software systems.

Now I want to help in our political system. I've seen how this world can marginalize people. I've been there myself. But I see the good in all of us. So that's what I want to bring to Congress. I want to bring us together. I want to help us move forward. I want to show the rest of the world what America is really about. It's about all of us. Not just the rich and powerful.

Oh yeah one last thing. The music on the site yeah I wrote that off the cuff.
